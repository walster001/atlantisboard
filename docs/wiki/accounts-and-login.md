# Signing in and your account

[← Wiki home](Home.md)

What you see on the sign-in page depends on **how your server was set up**. Your administrator might turn on email and password, **Sign in with Google**, or both.

---

## Email and password

Use your **email** and **password**, then **Sign in**. If **Remember me** is offered, it keeps you signed in longer on that browser (avoid on shared computers).

- **Sign up** — creates an account when self-registration is allowed.  
- **Forgot password** — starts a reset flow when your server sends email for that.

---

## Google

If a Google button appears, use it. You finish on Google’s screens, then return to Atlantisboard already signed in.

---

## Email verification and password reset

Follow links from **verification** or **password reset** emails when the app asks you to. If mail never arrives, check spam and ask your administrator to verify mail settings.

---

## After sign-in

You usually land on the **home** screen. If someone sent an **invite link**, you may see a short **accept invite** step first.

---

## Sign out

Open your **avatar menu** (top corner) and choose **Sign out** or **Log out**.

---

## First person on a new server

On a brand-new install, the **first registered account** is often treated as the **site administrator** so that person can finish setup for everyone else.
