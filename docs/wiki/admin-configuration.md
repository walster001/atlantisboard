# Admin configuration (full screen)

[← Wiki home](Home.md)

**Who:** people marked as **app administrators** on the server.

**Where:** open **Admin settings** from your avatar menu, or go to the admin configuration address your operator published.

This screen is split into broad areas such as **Configuration** (how the site behaves) and **Customisation** (how it looks). A sidebar lists sections — **login options**, **users**, **roles**, **branding**, **backups**, **monitoring**, **fonts**, and more.

---

## Login options

Turn **email sign-in** and/or **Google sign-in** on or off, and adjust related policies your admin cares about (lockouts, allowed methods, etc.). End users only see what you enable.

---

## Users and roles

- Browse **accounts** on the server.  
- Manage **permission roles** — which capabilities go with each role name.  
- Promote or demote **app admins** carefully; they can change everything.

---

## Branding

- **Login screen** — logo, colors, and wording for the sign-in page.  
- **App shell** — name and visuals elsewhere in the product.

---

## Backups and monitoring

- **Backups** — run or schedule copies of data to safe storage your admin configures.  
- **Monitor** — health-style panels (depends on what your install exposes).

---

## Custom fonts

Upload or select **fonts** used in rich text or themes according to your policy.

---
