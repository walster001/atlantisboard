# Admin configuration (full)

[← Wiki home](Home.md)

**Who:** people marked as **site** or **app** administrators.

**Where:** **Admin settings** in your avatar menu, or the URL your operator gives you.

The screen splits into **Configuration** (how the site behaves) and **Customisation** (how it looks). A sidebar lists sections.

---

## Configuration sections

Typical areas include:

- **General** — may be a placeholder on some versions.  
- **Login options** — which sign-in methods **appear to users** (email, Google, and related policies your org uses).  
- **Permissions** — roles and what each role may do.  
- **Users** — accounts on this server.  
- **Integrations** — may be a placeholder until finished.  
- **Backup** — run or schedule backups to safe storage.  
- **Monitor** — health-style information for operators.

---

## Customisation sections

- **Login branding** — logo and look of the sign-in page.  
- **App branding** — name and visuals in the main app.  
- **Custom fonts** — optional fonts for rich text or themes.  
- **Templates** — may be a placeholder until board or card templates ship fully.

---

## Placeholders

If a tab says it will arrive in a future update, believe it — use other sections or ask your developer for a roadmap.
