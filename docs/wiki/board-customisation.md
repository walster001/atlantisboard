# Board settings and look

[← Wiki home](Home.md)

**Board settings** apply to **one board**. Site-wide settings are under [Admin configuration](admin-configuration.md) for app administrators only.

Open **Board settings** from the board header when you have access. On phones the same content may appear as **steps** instead of one large panel.

---

## Board tab

Side areas often include:

- **Card defaults** — what new cards start with on this board.  
- **List settings** — behaviour and limits for lists.  
- **Labels** — create, rename, recolor, or remove labels for this board.

---

## Users and permissions

See **members** and their **roles**. Invite or remove people when allowed. User search may ask you to type a name or email and **confirm** to add someone.

---

## Theme and background

Pick a **preset look** or adjust **colors** and **background** for this board. Some servers allow a **custom theme editor** with a live preview while you edit.

---

## Audit log

The **Audit** tab shows **who changed what** on this board, when your role allows it. Use it to trace moves, edits, or membership changes.

---

## Invites (separate from settings)

**Invites** are often a separate action in the header — only about adding people, not colors or defaults.

---

## Missing tabs or buttons

If something is hidden, your **role** on that board does not include it. Ask a board admin to update your access.
