# Boards, lists, and cards

[← Wiki home](Home.md)

A **board** is the main working view: horizontal **lists** and **cards** inside each list.

---

## Lists

Lists are often stages like *To do* → *Doing* → *Done*, but you can name them anything. **Add list** is usually at the **far right** of the board: type a name and confirm.

You can **drag** a whole list sideways to change column order when you have permission.

---

## Cards

- **Add card** — at the bottom of a list; enter a **title** and submit.  
- **Open card** — click the card; it opens on top of the board.  
- **Links** — shared board links can open a specific card automatically when your admin supports that style of link.

---

## Drag and drop

Drag a card **up or down** in a list to reorder. Drag a card **into another list** to move it. Empty lists still accept drops so you do not have to create a dummy card first.

On small screens, the board may let you **swipe** between lists instead of showing every column at once.

---

## Board header

Depending on your role you may see:

- **Back** to home  
- **Board settings** — labels, members, theme, audit log  
- **Invites** — separate flow for inviting people  
- **Your avatar menu** — profile, sign out, and **site admin** links only if you are that kind of admin  

---

## If the board is missing

**Board not found** or being sent home usually means the link is wrong, the board was removed, or you do not have access. Ask a board admin to re-invite you or check the link.
