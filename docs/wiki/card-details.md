# Card details: everything inside a card

[← Wiki home](Home.md)

Opening a card shows a **side panel** (or full overlay on small screens) with sections you can scroll. Not every section is required — empty ones stay subtle until you add something.

---

## Title and description

- Change the **title** inline.  
- **Description** supports rich formatting (headings, lists, links, images depending on what your admin allows). Look for “add a description” when it is empty.

---

## People

- **Assignees** — pick who is responsible. Search fields often work by typing a name or email and pressing **Enter** to add.

---

## Dates

- Cards can track **start**, **due**, and **end** style dates when your board uses them — handy for planning windows, not only deadlines.

---

## Labels

- Colored **labels** help scanning the board. Labels are usually managed in **board settings**; on the card you toggle which labels apply.

---

## Checklists

- Add **checklists** and **items**; tick items off as you go. Good for acceptance criteria or subtasks without spawning extra cards.

---

## Comments

- **Thread** discussion on the card. When others are typing, you may see light **typing** indicators so you know a reply is coming.

---

## Attachments

- **Upload files** attached to the card. Size limits depend on server policy.  
- Images and documents show as a list you can open or remove according to your permissions.

---

## Reminders

- If enabled, set **reminders** so you get nudged before something matters.

---

## Closing the card

Use **Close**, **Escape**, or click outside the panel — whichever the UI offers on your device.
