# Inside a card

[← Wiki home](Home.md)

Opening a card shows a **panel** you can scroll. Sections stay minimal until you add content.

---

## Title and description

Edit the **title** directly. The **description** supports rich text (headings, lists, links, images depending on what your organization allows). Empty descriptions show a hint like “add a description.”

---

## People

**Assignees** are the people responsible for the card. Picking names often uses a search field: type, then confirm the person you want.

---

## Labels

**Labels** are colored tags for scanning the board. Creating or renaming labels is usually done in **board settings**; on the card you toggle which labels apply.

---

## Dates

Cards can track **start**, **due**, and similar dates when your board uses them.

---

## Checklists

Add **checklists** and line items; check them off as work completes.

---

## Comments

Discuss the card in a **comment thread**. You may see light indicators when someone else is typing.

---

## Attachments

**Upload files** attached to the card. Size limits depend on server policy.

---

## Reminders

If enabled, set **reminders** so you get nudges before something matters.

---

## Closing the card

Use **Close**, tap outside, or **Escape** — whichever the UI shows on your device.
