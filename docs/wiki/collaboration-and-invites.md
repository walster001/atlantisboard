# Invites and working with others

[← Wiki home](Home.md)

---

## Board invites

Someone with invite rights opens **Invites** from the board header, creates a link or invitation, and sends it to you. When you open the link while signed in, the app **accepts** the invite and drops you back on the home hub (or shows a clear error if the link expired).

---

## Roles

Roles are labels like **Admin**, **Manager**, **Member**, and **Viewer** on a board or workspace. What each role can do is decided on your server — if you are unsure, ask your board admin.

**App administrators** are different: they can change **site-wide** settings for everyone. You might see **Admin settings** in your avatar menu only if you are that kind of admin.

---

## Real-time behaviour

When you and a teammate are on the **same board**, moves and many edits show up for both of you without a manual refresh. If something looks stuck, a refresh usually clears it; persistent problems are for your server admin.

---

## Mentions and notifications

Short **toast** messages appear for saves, errors, and long tasks like imports. A full “notification center” may be minimal today — watch the toasts and email from your org for important events.
