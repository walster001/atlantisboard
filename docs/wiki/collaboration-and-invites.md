# Invites and teamwork

[← Wiki home](Home.md)

---

## Board invites

Someone with invite rights opens **Invites** from the board header, creates a link or invitation, and sends it to you. After you open it while signed in, the app accepts you or shows a clear error (for example if the link expired).

---

## Roles

Roles such as **Admin**, **Manager**, **Member**, or **Viewer** control what you can change. Exact names and powers depend on your server — ask your board admin if unsure.

**Site administrators** are different from **board** admins: they can change settings for the **entire product** for all users.

---

## Live updates

When teammates change cards or lists you are allowed to see, updates can appear **without refreshing**. If something looks stuck, one manual refresh often fixes it; ongoing problems belong with your server administrator.

---

## Toasts and messages

Short **popup messages** confirm saves, warn about errors, or show import progress. They are not a full notification inbox by themselves.
