# What this app is

[← Wiki home](Home.md)

Atlantisboard is a **self-hosted** app for planning work on **boards**. Each board has **lists** (columns) and lists hold **cards**. A card is usually one task or one idea you can open for more detail.

**Workspaces** group boards on your **home** screen so personal and team work stay organized.

When the product talks about **live** or **real-time** updates, it means changes from teammates can appear on your screen without you manually refreshing the page.

---

## Words you will see

| Word | Meaning |
|------|--------|
| Board | One canvas of lists and cards. |
| List / column | A vertical stack of cards; often a workflow stage. |
| Card | One item; open it for description, checklists, files, and more. |
| Workspace | A labeled group of boards on the home screen. |
| Invite | A way to add someone to a board or workspace with a chosen role. |
