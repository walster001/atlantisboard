# Home screen

[← Wiki home](Home.md)

The **home** screen lists your **workspaces** and the **boards** inside them. From here you open boards, create workspaces or boards (when allowed), and may start an **import**.

---

## First visit

If nothing exists yet, the page explains creating a workspace and adding boards. That is normal for a new account.

---

## Workspaces

Each workspace is a **section** with its own title. Some workspaces are only **shared** with you; you might not be able to add boards there. When you can add boards, look for a **plus** or **add** control near the workspace title.

---

## Board tiles

Click a **board** tile to open it. If you are allowed, you can **drag** tiles to reorder them or move them between workspaces.

---

## Workspace page in the address bar

If you open a link that looks like a dedicated **workspace** page and it says **placeholder**, use the **home** screen instead for the same boards until that page is fully built.

---

## Import

When your role allows, you can open **import** from the home area to bring in data from supported sources. Large imports may take a minute; wait for a clear success or error message.
