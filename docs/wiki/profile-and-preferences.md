# Profile, language, and avatar

[← Wiki home](Home.md)

Open your **avatar menu** (top corner) and choose **Profile**.

---

## What you can change

- **Display name** — how teammates see you.  
- **Avatar** — upload a picture or remove one; some servers host the image file for you with size and type rules shown on the form.  
- **Language** — pick the interface language when translations are available.  
- Optional checkboxes for **autodetect** and **translate user-created interface** may appear depending on your server — ask your admin what they recommend.

---

## Theme (light / dark)

The overall app may follow **light**, **dark**, or **match the device** settings. **Per-board** colors and backgrounds are separate — those live under [Board customisation](board-customisation.md).
