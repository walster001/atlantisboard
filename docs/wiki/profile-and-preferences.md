# Profile and preferences

[← Wiki home](Home.md)

Open your **avatar menu** and choose **Profile** (or similar).

---

## What you can usually change

- **Display name** — how others see you.  
- **Avatar** — upload or remove a photo; the form explains allowed types and sizes.  
- **Language** — interface language when translations exist.  
- Extra toggles may appear for **language detection** or **translation** — ask your admin what they recommend for your organization.

---

## Light and dark mode

The app may follow **light**, **dark**, or **match the device**. **Per-board** colors and backgrounds are separate and live under [Board settings](board-customisation.md).
